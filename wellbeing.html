The Brain on Junk Food

The brain requires high-quality fuel to function. Studies show that diets high in refined sugars can impair brain function and even worsen symptoms of mood disorders.

Academic Impacts

Diets high in saturated fats can actually hamper the hippocampus, the part of the brain responsible for memory. Students often experience "brain fog" and decreased alertness after consuming high-sugar lunches.

Mental Health Links

  • Anxiety: Caffeine and sugar crashes can mimic or worsen anxiety symptoms.
  • Depression: Long-term poor nutrition is linked to higher risks of clinical depression in teenagers.
