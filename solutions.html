Real Solutions for Real Schools

The goal isn't just to remove "bad" food, but to foster a culture of wellness. Here is how schools can make a difference:

1. Policy Change

Implementing strict nutrition standards for vending machines and cafeteria menus.

2. Nutrition Education

Integrating food science and cooking classes into the curriculum to empower students to make their own healthy choices.

3. Better Alternatives

Replacing sodas with infused water and chips with fresh fruit or nuts in accessible areas.

4. Family Involvement

Educating parents on packing healthy lunches through school newsletters and workshops.
