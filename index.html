The Hidden Crisis in Our Cafeterias

In modern schools, the accessibility of ultra-processed junk food has reached an all-time high. While convenient, the excessive consumption of high-sugar, high-fat snacks is creating a silent health crisis among students.

The Triple Threat

  • Physical: Increasing rates of childhood obesity and early-onset type 2 diabetes.
  • Mental: Fluctuating energy levels leading to mood swings and anxiety.
  • Academic: Poor nutrition directly correlates with lower concentration and test scores.
